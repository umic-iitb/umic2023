Project information

  • Competition: IGVC 2017
  • Organizer: Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition
  • Project date: June, 2017

We also participated in IGVC in 2017, where we were declared the world champions. This involved developing our own software to autonomously navigate our bot, and then researching the hardware requirements and manufacturing it from scratch. It became a major landmark for our team and something which we aspire to achieve again. It gained us some huge recognition and opened our horizons to more challenging problems.

We had to travel to Michigan, USA to participate which involved travel and accommodation costs for the entire competition. We had to disassemble the bot from here and then assemble it back after transporting it from India which was a herculean task logistically.
