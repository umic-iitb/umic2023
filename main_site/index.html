AeRoVe

On a never-ending pursuit of developing an ultimate system of autonomous multirotor and fixed-wing aircraft.


At Team AeRoVe, we always look for problems that challenge, excite, and motivate us to grow intellectually. Our purpose is to further the boundaries of autonomous aerial technology and realize the true potential of this field. We aim to contribute and start the culture of creating cutting edge technology through indigenous innovation.

Incorporating different subsystems namely ‘Mechatronics’, ‘Avionics','Controls’, ‘Machine Learning’, ‘Localisation’, ‘Path Planning’, and ‘Localisation’, the team emphatically covers every aspect to forge a de rigueur system of autonomous aerial vehicles.

SeDriCa

On a mission to achieve Level 5 autonomy, capable of autonomous driving under Indian road conditions


SeDriCa research project aims to develop India's first self-driving car, trying to achieve Level 5 autonomy, capable of autonomous driving under Indian road conditions. The current testing is being done on Mahindra e2o and the plans include further and more precise improvements in the software stack as well as the hardware design.

Our current goal is to have an autonomous vehicle that can successfully be run inside a geofenced arena like our institute. This would include proper lane following, staying within safe velocity bounds, proper braking, efficiently handling intersections and tackling other road obstacles.
