Project information

  • Competition: International Aerial Robotics Hardware Challenge
  • Organizer: IARC
  • Project date: 2022

In 2022, we set out on the largest project our team had undertaken in its history. For the IARC mission 9 hardware challenge, we built a drone which was capable of flying 3km out at sea, rendezvousing with an enemy ship, grabbing onto a swaying mast and replacing a communication module attached to it. Our drone weighed just under 18kg and had a diagonal span of around 1.8 metres. It would take off coupled to a smaller drone which would then decouple mid-flight to fly back to base.
